The Ultimate Adrenaline Rush

Hawaii's shark diving tours take you 3 miles offshore into crystal-clear waters where Galapagos, sandbar, and tiger sharks patrol. Whether you choose the safety of a cage or the thrill of open water freediving, you'll witness these magnificent predators up close.
