Can You Visit Multiple Islands?

Yes, but don't over-pack your itinerary.

  • 7 days: Stick to one island
  • 10 days: Two islands work well
  • 14+ days: Three islands possible

Inter-island flights are cheap ($50-100) and quick (30-45 minutes). But factor in airport timeâ€”you'll lose half a day per island hop. Better to deeply explore fewer islands than skim multiple.
