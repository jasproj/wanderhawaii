Hawaii Helicopter Tours: See Paradise from Above

Hidden waterfalls, volcanic craters & sea cliffs

Nothing reveals Hawaii's dramatic beauty like a helicopter tour. Soar over volcanic craters, hidden waterfalls inaccessible by foot, and coastlines that look like they belong in Jurassic Park (because they were filmed there). Each island offers unique aerial perspectives you simply can't get any other way.

Which Island Has the Best Helicopter Tour?

Island Highlights Best For
Kauai Na Pali Coast, Waimea Canyon, Mt. Waialeale waterfalls Most dramatic scenery; #1 overall
Big Island Active volcanoes, lava flows, diverse landscapes Volcanic activity, unique terrain
Maui Haleakala crater, West Maui waterfalls, Hana coast Variety of landscapes
Oahu Ko'olau cliffs, Sacred Falls, North Shore Convenient from Waikiki

Our pick: Kauai offers the most jaw-dropping helicopter tour in Hawaii. Much of the island is inaccessible by road, and the combination of Na Pali's sea cliffs, Waimea Canyon's colors, and Mt. Waialeale's hundreds of waterfalls is unmatched.

Doors-Off vs. Doors-On

Doors-Off Tours

  • Unobstructed views and photos‚Äîno reflections
  • More thrilling, immersive experience
  • Wind and noise (you'll wear a harness)
  • Not available on all operators
  • Slightly higher price

Doors-On Tours

  • Climate-controlled, quieter cabin
  • Better for nervous flyers or children
  • Photos through windows (can get reflections)
  • More widely available

Recommendation: If you're comfortable and want the best photos, doors-off is worth it. For families or nervous flyers, doors-on is still spectacular.

  • Duration: 45-65 minutes

  • Duration: 45-120 minutes
  • Must-see: Kilauea volcano, lava fields, Kohala waterfalls

  • Must-see: Ko'olau cliffs, Sacred Falls, North Shore

Tips for the Best Experience

  • Book morning flights: Clearer skies before afternoon clouds build
  • Wear dark clothing: Reduces window reflections for photos
  • Request front seat: Best views (usually costs extra or luck of the draw)
  • Take motion sickness meds: If you're prone to it
  • Charge your camera: You'll want lots of photos
  • Listen to the pilot: They provide fascinating narration

How Much Do Helicopter Tours Cost?

  • Budget tours: $200-250 (shorter flights, doors-on)
  • Standard tours: $300-400 (45-60 min, quality operators)
  • Premium tours: $400-600+ (doors-off, longer flights, landing tours)

Bottom Line

A helicopter tour is a splurge‚Äîbut it's often the highlight of a Hawaii trip. You'll see waterfalls, valleys, and coastlines impossible to reach any other way, with memories (and photos) that last forever.
