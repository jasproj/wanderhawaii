High-Speed Ocean Adventure

Jet skiing in Hawaii is an unmatched adrenaline rush. Race across crystal-clear water with Diamond Head as your backdrop, explore coastlines inaccessible by land, and spot sea turtles and dolphins from your waverunner.

No license or experience needed‚Äîoperators provide safety briefings and instruction. Rentals run 30 minutes to 2 hours, with guided tours and freestyle options available. Single and tandem jet skis let you ride solo or share the experience.
