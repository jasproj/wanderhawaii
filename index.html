Hawaii offers incredible adventures for every traveler! Top activities include snorkeling at Molokini Crater (Maui), helicopter tours over active volcanoes (Big Island), surfing lessons on the North Shore (Oahu), attending a traditional luau, hiking Diamond Head, swimming with manta rays at night (Kona), and boat tours along the Na Pali Coast (Kauai). What should I do if it's raining in Hawaii?

Rain in Hawaii is usually brief and localized! If one side of an island is rainy, the other side is often sunny. Great rainy day activities include: visiting museums (Pearl Harbor, Bishop Museum), spa treatments, shopping, indoor luaus, or simply driving to the sunny side of the island. Hawaii is great year-round! Peak season (Dec-Mar) offers whale watching and slightly cooler weather. Summer (Jun-Aug) has calmer waters for snorkeling. Shoulder seasons (Apr-May, Sep-Nov) offer lower prices and fewer crowds. Water temperature stays 75-80Â°F year-round.
