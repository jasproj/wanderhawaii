Fly Through Paradise

Hawaii's ziplines aren't just about adrenaline‚Äîthey're about experiencing landscapes inaccessible any other way. Glide over valleys once reserved for Hawaiian royalty, past waterfalls hidden in private land, and through canopies alive with tropical birds.

Courses range from beginner-friendly (shorter lines, lower heights) to extreme adventures with lines over a mile long and speeds exceeding 60 mph. Most include multiple ziplines, suspension bridges, and hiking through pristine forest.

No experience necessary. Expert guides handle all the equipment; you just enjoy the ride. Weight limits typically range from 75-275 lbs depending on the course.
