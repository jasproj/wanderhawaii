What to Expect

  • Duration: Most cruises are 2-3 hours
  • Drinks: Usually included (check what's premium vs. house)
  • Food: Ranges from pupus (appetizers) to full dinners
  • Seasickness: Catamarans are stable; take Dramamine if sensitive
  • What to bring: Light jacket (ocean breeze), camera, cash for tips

Romantic Options

For couples celebrating anniversaries or honeymoons, look for:

  • Private or semi-private charters
  • Dinner cruises with assigned seating
  • Smaller boats (under 30 passengers)
  • Cruises with live music
