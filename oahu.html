Why Visit Oahu?

Oahu is where most Hawaiian adventures begin. As the most accessible island, it offers the perfect introduction to island life. Start with a sunrise hike up Diamond Head, snorkel with sea turtles at Hanauma Bay, and watch surfers tackle massive waves on the North Shore.

History buffs will find Pearl Harbor moving and educational. Foodies will discover incredible diversity from food trucks to fine dining. And with 329 tour options, there's something for every type of traveler.
